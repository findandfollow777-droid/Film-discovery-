How to Play

1
Spot the Film

You're shown 10 still images from a movie franchise. For each image, identify which of the 3 films it's from.

2
Beat the Clock

You have 30 seconds for all 10 shots. Speed matters for tiebreaking among top scores!

3
Build Streaks

Consecutive correct answers build a streak. Each streak of length n scores n² × 10 points — retroactively!
