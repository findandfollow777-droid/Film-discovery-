    How to Play

    A Top 10 list is missing its 10th movie. Your job: figure out which movie it is.

    1. Read the clue and guess the missing movie.
    2. Wrong guess? Pick a face-down tile from the 3×3 grid.
    3. Answer the tile's trivia question to reveal its movie poster.
    4. Get a wrong answer? You can retry once next round with an easier question.
    5. Each round gives you a new, easier clue about the missing movie.
    6. After round 9, you get one final guess with the easiest clue.
